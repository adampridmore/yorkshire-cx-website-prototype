New to Cyclo-Cross?

Everything you need to know about getting started with Yorkshire's favourite cycling sport.

What is cyclo-cross?

Cyclo-cross is off-road bike racing on short circuits — parks, fields, and woodland — that mix grass, mud, sand, and small obstacles. Races last under an hour. There is no long-distance suffering — just intense, exciting racing with a great community around it.

Why you'll love it

⏱

Short, sharp races

Under an hour. You won't be there all day — and you'll be wondering when the next race is.

🌞

Year-round racing

The league runs a Summer Series (May–August) and a Winter Series (October–February) — something exciting to train for all year.

👥

Race at your own level

Age-graded categories from Under 8 to Veteran 60+. You're always racing people at a similar level.

🚲

Any bike will do

Road, mountain, gravel or CX — beginners regularly turn up on whatever they have and have a great time.

🎉

Real race atmosphere

Mass starts, a hooter, a crowd, and your name called at the finish. It feels like proper sport.

🙂

Genuinely welcoming

The YCCA has been running races for decades. Everyone remembers their first race — you'll be looked after.
