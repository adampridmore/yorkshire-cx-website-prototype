Race schedule

Practical information

⌛

Sign-on times

Sign-on opens 90 minutes before your race. Arrive with time to spare — queues build up close to race time.

🛌

Parking

Some venues charge £3 for parking. Check the individual event details for your venue.

🍔

Catering

A coffee van with cakes is available at most venues.
