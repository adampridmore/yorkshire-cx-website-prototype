Why cyclo-cross?

A sport that welcomes everyone — whatever your bike, your fitness, or your experience.

⏱

Under an hour

Short, sharp races that don't take all day. No all-day commitment needed.

👥

All ages, all abilities

Under 8s to veterans — age-graded categories mean you always have people to race.

🚲

Any bike

Road, mountain, gravel or dedicated CX machine — start with what you have.

🎉

Real race atmosphere

Mass starts, crowd support, proper results. It feels like proper sport from race one.
